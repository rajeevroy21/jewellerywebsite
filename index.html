24 April 2024 / Cleaning
How to clean/Maintain Our jewellery?

Maintaining the luster of your rose gold jewelry is essential for preserving its beauty. To clean your pieces, gently scrub them with a soft-bristled brush and mild soapy water, then rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a soft cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasive materials, as they can damage the delicate surface of rose gold. Regular cleaning and occasional polishing will ensure that your rose gold jewelry retains its radiant shine, allowing you to enjoy its timeless beauty for years to come.
